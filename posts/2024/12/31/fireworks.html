Fireworks

12/31/24

We used to light fireworks with dad. Simple fun. We never did big displays, always prefering cheap, small fare. Our favorite item was a strobe-light, which is a magnesium strip that flashes silently as it burns, and it burns long and hot. We used to take the husks of little paper-tanks and light them on fire with strobes. Drop them into the spent tubes. Toss them into the ditch to see if they burn underwater. The fires were more interesting than the actual fireworks.

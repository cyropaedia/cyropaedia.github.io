Mario Galaxy

12/27/24

My brother Johnny and I get to hang out this time of year for weeks instead of days, so we usually pick an old game and run through it again. Unusual choice this year in Super Mario Galaxy, which Johnny never played growing up, though Thomas and I finished it when it first came out.

The ghost levels are stand-outs in quality. I remember enjoying the lava levels more than I actually do now. Flying is harder than I remember it, but still fun.

I'll miss Johnny and Megan when they leave. The first half of the year is the time I see them least. Will visit for Mardi Gras. Francis will be six months older by the time I can spend more than a weekend of time with him, though.

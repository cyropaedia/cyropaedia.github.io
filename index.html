Constellations

Astronomy is a cultural universal; everyone does it. It's easy to think of it as being a difficulty science for the big-brains to think about, but just looking at the stars and moon is astronomy. No need to gatekeep it.

Constellations are fun, because they come with stories. In modern urban nations, too many people know constellations only from line diagrams in a textbook. Kills the joy of it. They look so much better and more convincing as images when you see them in the real sky. The outlines don't do them justice; even the middle stars contribute to the image. If you haven't seen it, make a drive out this weekend to a dark area and take a look.
